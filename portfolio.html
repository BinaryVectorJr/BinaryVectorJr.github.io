Cabin in the Woods

Unreal Engine 4

A small level design project where I tried to include different types of terrain and foliage, in the level. It also includes a log cabin, as well as some smoke and fire particle effects. The First Person Shooter template has been used here, so as to make the player better experience the environment, as well as move about in it freely.

Master Bedroom

Trimble SketchUp | Unreal Engine 4

A digital recreation of a bedroom of a house. The initial design was done in Trimble SketchUp, and then the projet was imported into Unreal Studio using the new “.udatasmith” extension. Most of the objects in the room (apart from the bed, ottoman, and the phone) have been designed by me in SketchUp.

Escape the Room

Unreal Engine 4

A simple game where the user has to figure out how to escape two rooms. The main mechanic used in the game is putting a specific amount of weight onto the marked spots so as to “open” the door. The tables and chairs have a mass to them, and the “spot” is a trigger volume. Also, through this project I was trying to understand the basics of a grabbing mechanism, as well as the implementation of physics in video games.

Jungle Run

Unreal Engine 4 | Adobe Photoshop

A simple 2D sidescroller game that mimics the look and feel of classic 8-bit sidescrollers such as Metroid and Super Mario. This is one of the levels I am focussing on, and my goal is to publish this into a full fledged game. Most of the level assets have been done by me as a mini-project to try and learn pixel art. Free assets off the internet have been used for backgrounds, the coin sprites, and the player sprite.

Lost in Space

Unity3D

This virtual reality demo was created as a part of the edX course I did, entitled “Creating Virtual Reality Applications” (UCSanDiegoX:CSE190x). The application focusses on two parts - create game objects in a virtual room and manipulate them (along with existing objects in the scene). The user is able to freely create objects and them relocate, rotate, and scale them. ALso there are three different modes of locomotion - teleport, walk, and an automatic hover mode.
